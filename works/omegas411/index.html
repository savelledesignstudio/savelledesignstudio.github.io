Omegas411

Branding and landing page for a comprehensive Omega-3 essential fatty acids resource guide.

The Brief

Role

Designer & Developer

Timeline

2025

Services

Branding, Web Design, Development, SEO

I designed the branding and a landing page for a resource guide to Omega-3 essential fatty acids. The project was designed in Figma and developed using Tailwind CSS, with a focus on creating trust and credibility.

The landing page hosts a list of hand-selected videos by subject matter experts, research articles, and studies to help spread awareness of Omega-3 essential fatty acids. I designed the logo to command trust and implemented SEO best practices along with Google Analytics to track engagement and reach.
