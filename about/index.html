About Me

I'm Savelle McThias, a designer and developer based in Scottsdale, Arizona. I help growing businesses create beautiful, effective websites that connect with customers and drive real results.

With 18+ years of experience, I've had the privilege of working with companies ranging from small startups to major brands. Along the way, I've learned what really matters: clear communication, thoughtful design, and solutions that work for your business and your customers.

Today, I focus on helping small to medium-sized businesses with landing pages, website refreshes, brand touchups, and email campaigns. My goal is simple: create design that looks professional, works smoothly, and helps your business grow.
