Web Design & Development

Young Living

News and promotion hub for Young Living distributors showcasing products and lifestyle content.

The Brief

Role

UX Designer & Developer

Timeline

2022

Services

Web Design, UX Design, Development

I designed a news and promotion hub for Young Living distributors called Grow News. This platform gave tips on product usage, featured lifestyle articles, and shared company news to engage their massive distributor network.

I was able to capture the essence of their branding while creating a responsive website blog that was easy to navigate and engaging for their audience. I wireframed and designed the entire experience from concept to launch.

The site launched using their holiday kit, reaching over 6 million global Brand Partners and marketing 100+ products through engaging content and strategic promotions.
