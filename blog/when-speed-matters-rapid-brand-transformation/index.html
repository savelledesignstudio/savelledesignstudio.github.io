When Speed Matters: Rapid Brand Transformation

How to deliver a complete branding revamp and website in just 5 days without compromising quality.

Five days. That’s all the time I had to revamp Nikkl’s branding and build their website from scratch.

Nikkl is a fintech startup helping unicorn employees unlock liquidity from their stock options. They needed to launch fast, but they couldn’t afford to look rushed. The solution? Efficient collaboration and laser focus on what matters.

When Tight Deadlines Meet High Expectations

Day 1-2: Branding Revamp I redesigned their logo and modernized their color scheme, creating a look that feels both trustworthy and innovative—exactly what a fintech startup needs.

Day 3-4: Design & Development Working directly with their Chief of Marketing and Chief of Technology, I designed and developed a website that explains complex financial opportunities in simple terms. Custom illustrations helped break down intimidating concepts into accessible visuals.

Day 5: Polish & Launch Final refinements, testing, and we were live.

Making Complexity Simple

The biggest challenge wasn’t the timeline—it was making stock options understandable to the 260,000 unicorn employees in America. Good design simplifies. Great design educates while it simplifies.

The Takeaway

Fast doesn’t have to mean sloppy. With clear communication, focused priorities, and the right expertise, rapid delivery is possible without sacrificing quality. Nikkl got their rebrand and website in 5 days, but more importantly, they got a platform that clearly communicates their value to their target audience.

Sometimes speed is a requirement, not a compromise.
