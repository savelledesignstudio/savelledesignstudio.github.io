Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A typical website project takes 4-8 weeks from initial consultation to launch. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial discussion.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes! I work with clients worldwide and have established processes for remote collaboration, including regular video calls, project management tools, and clear communication.

What information do you need to get started?

To begin, I'll need an overview of your project goals, target audience, timeline, and budget. Any existing brand materials, content, or examples of designs you like are also helpful.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes, I offer maintenance and support packages for websites and ongoing design services for clients who need regular design work. We can discuss options that fit your needs.
