Brand Identity Touchups

Refine and modernize your brand's visual identity to create a cohesive, professional look that resonates with your audience.

What is a Brand Touchup?

You already have a brand, but it might need some polish. Maybe your logo feels dated, your colors don't work well together, or you're not sure how to use your brand consistently across different materials.

A brand identity touchup refreshes and refines your existing brand elements, creating a cohesive visual system that makes your business look professional and trustworthy.

What's Included

Logo Refinement

Polish your existing logo or create variations that work better in different contexts.

Color Palette

Refine your brand colors to ensure they work well together and across all materials.

Typography Selection

Choose fonts that reflect your brand personality and ensure readability.

Brand Guidelines

A simple guide showing how to use your brand elements consistently.

Marketing Templates

Social media graphics, business cards, or other templates you need.

File Delivery

All your brand assets in the right formats for print and digital use.

Why Consistent Branding Matters

Build Trust

A consistent, professional brand makes your business appear more credible and established.

Stand Out

Strong visual identity helps you stand out from competitors and be memorable.

Save Time

Clear guidelines and templates make it faster to create marketing materials.
